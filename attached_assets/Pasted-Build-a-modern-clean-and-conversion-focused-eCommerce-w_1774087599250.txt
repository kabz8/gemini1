Build a modern, clean, and conversion-focused eCommerce website for a medical supplies brand called “Gemini Surgicals” based in Nairobi, Kenya.
🎨 Design Style
Minimal, clean, and clinical aesthetic (inspired by high-end medical brands)
Use a white base, with blue (#1E88E5 / #1565C0) and soft gradient accents (blue → purple) similar to the social media posts
Smooth gradients for offer sections (like weekly offers banners)
Rounded cards, soft shadows, spacious layout
Clear product focus (large product images, simple layouts)
UI should feel like a trusted healthcare brand
🔤 Typography (IMPORTANT)
Use modern geometric sans fonts similar to Inter / DM Sans but NOT those
Use:
Primary Font: Plus Jakarta Sans
Secondary Font: General Sans or Satoshi
Fallback: system-ui, sans-serif
Typography style:
Bold headings
Clean readable body text
Slightly tight letter spacing for headings
